The Adventures
of Maggie May and Piccolo Too!

WELCOME NEW FRIENDS! Our project is a series of children's book written by Frank McSorley and illustrated by Shelby Van Cleef. The series follows an old, forgotten van (Maggie May) and her rescue dog (Piccolo) as they overcome life's challenges with friendship and self-acceptance.
