The Adventures
of Maggie May and Piccolo Too!

The Adventures of Maggie May and Piccolo Too! is a children's book written by Frank McSorley and illustrated by Shelby Van Cleef. The series follows an old, forgotten van (Maggie May) and her rescue dog (Piccolo) as they overcome life's challenges with friendship and self-acceptance. The van and the dog are based on Frank's real rescue dog and restored VW van. Frank's son Joshua discovered Maggie May and dreamed of getting her back on the road before leaving his life to go work with the Master Mechanic on January 4, 2010.
