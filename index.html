Our Project

The Adventures of Maggie May and Piccolo Too! is a children's book written by Frank McSorley and illustrated by Shelby Van Cleef. The series follows an old, forgotten VW van (Maggie May) and her rescue dog (Piccolo) as they overcome life's challenges with friendship and self-acceptance. The van and the dog are based on Frank's real rescue dog and restored VW van. Frank's son Joshua discovered Maggie May and dreamed of getting her back on the road before leaving his life to go work with the Master Mechanic on January 4, 2010.

Maggie's Travels

Maggie May was discovered by Frank's son Joshua over ten years ago on an old forgotten car lot. It was his dream to bring Maggie home, restore her and travel on adventures cross country. Come see some of the places Maggie has been!

Piccolo's Corner

Piccolo lived on the streets of New Brunswick, New Jersey for 8-9 months before meeting Maggie. Now, the two are travel together on all of their adventures. Check out a day in the life of Piccolo!
